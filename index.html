The trajectory

  • MusicianTouring, recording, released on Cleopatra Records
  • Hairstylist10 years building a loyal clientele in Asheville, NC
  • ScreenwriterFull Sail University, valedictorian, LA screenplay finalist
  • CopywriterBEDGEAR — brand voice, campaign ideation, channel execution
  • Content SpecialistFull-funnel marketing, 60% YoY e-commerce growth

I've spent my career learning how to make people feel something — from a stage, a salon chair, a screenplay, and now a brief. Every version of that work comes down to the same thing: understanding your audience well enough to say exactly what they need to hear.

At BEDGEAR, I helped drive 60% year-over-year e-commerce growth through campaigns spanning paid media, email, organic social, retail, and web. I don't just write — I think in channels, build in brand voice, and stay focused on what moves the needle.

I'm originally from Asheville. I care about community, craft, and brands that actually mean something to the people who love them. That's the kind of work I want to do more of.

Let's make something worth reading.
