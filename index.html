About

Am I actually a hypochondriac if it's self-diagnosed?
The movie Mean Streets could've been called Bullyvards.
Trash is ashtray in Pig Latin.

I'm Stephen, and these are the kind of thoughts that go through my head.

Turns out there's a job for people who think like that.

As a creative copywriter and storyteller, I channel the same curiosity and wordplay into building brand voice, developing campaigns, and writing compelling copy that converts across paid media, social, email, retail, and web. On brief, on brand, and on time.

My first publication came at thirteen. A poem about my favorite guitar. Eventually, those two interests merged, and I started writing songs. In 2020, my band Ugly Runner released a record on Cleopatra Records, which remains the biggest checkmark on my bucket list. Before marketing, I worked as a hairstylist in Asheville, North Carolina. Connecting with clients, understanding what they want, and delivering results in real time was some of the most valuable training I could have asked for as a marketer. These days, when I'm not working, you can find me hanging out in the city, riding motorcycles, playing guitar, or watching baseball (Go Braves) with my wife Michelle, our dog Charlie, and our cat Sam.
